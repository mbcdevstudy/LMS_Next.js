Create a modern, high-converting landing page design for an LMS (Learning Management System) platform.

Style:
- Futuristic neon cyber theme
- Dark background with glowing gradients (blue, purple, pink neon accents)
- Glassmorphism cards
- Soft glowing shadows
- Smooth UI
- Clean modern typography (Inter / Poppins style)
- Subtle animated glow effects
- Professional SaaS feel

Brand name: EduNova

Target audience:
Students, developers, and professionals who want to learn tech skills online.

Sections required:

1) Hero Section:
- Strong headline: "Upgrade Your Skills. Unlock Your Future."
- Short subheading about interactive online courses
- CTA buttons: "Start Learning" (primary neon glow) and "Explore Courses" (outline glow)
- Right side illustration: futuristic dashboard mockup with neon highlights
- Background glowing gradient or abstract tech shapes

2) Trusted By Section:
- Logos of companies (placeholder logos in grayscale neon hover effect)

3) Features Section (3–6 cards):
- Interactive Learning
- Progress Tracking Dashboard
- Certificates
- Community Support
- AI Personalized Learning
- Mobile Friendly
Each card should have neon border glow and hover animation.

4) Popular Courses Section:
- 3–6 course cards
- Course thumbnail
- Title
- Instructor
- Rating
- Price
- Enroll button with glow effect

5) How It Works (3 steps):
- Sign Up
- Choose Course
- Start Learning
Use minimal glowing icons and step indicators.

6) Testimonials Section:
- 3 student reviews
- Profile image circle
- Name + Role
- Short review
- Neon glass card style

7) Pricing Section:
- 3 pricing tiers (Basic, Pro, Premium)
- Highlight middle plan with stronger neon glow
- Monthly / Yearly toggle UI

8) FAQ Section:
- Collapsible accordion
- Smooth glow expand animation

9) Final CTA Section:
- Big glowing banner
- Headline: "Start Your Learning Journey Today"
- CTA button with strong neon glow

10) Footer:
- Logo
- Navigation links
- Social media icons with glow hover
- Newsletter subscription field

Design Requirements:
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Clean spacing
- Modern SaaS layout
- High contrast readability
- No cartoonish elements
- No nano banana style
- Professional tech startup aesthetic
